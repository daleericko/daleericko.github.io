Meet your instructor

Hi - I'm Erick!

I have worked as a data scientist for years, and the most rewarding part of my job is watching the my students' joy as they master a new concept and run code for the first time. The idea behind these courses was to provide more of that joy to more people.

In college, I was so captivated by what could be done through code that I spent my free time reading and watching tutorials about coding and data science. But these generic videos and tutorials often left me more confused since I couldn't ask anyone a question if something didn't make sense. And these videos left out helpful tips that could have saved me time and energy. With this in mind, I developed a curriculum with individualized lessons to ensure that you will learn all the essentials of coding and data science-from someone who uses these techniques every day-without all the stress.
